# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 27, 2026**

This Privacy Policy applies to the Minimal Piano application ("App").

## 1. Data Collection

The Minimal Piano App is designed to be a standalone, local application. 
- **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.**
- The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any identifying details.
- The App does not track your usage patterns, keystrokes, or musical notes played.

## 2. Local Storage

The App may use your device's local storage solely to save your local preferences (such as your current octave or zoom level). This data remains entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third parties.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This App is built using open-source frameworks (React Native and Expo). We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that collect data about you.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for children. Since we do not collect any personal data, we inadvertently comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar global regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact the developer.
